Problem Statement

ConnectTel faces monthly churn and seeks a data-driven way to identify customers at risk before they leave.

Dataset

The project uses telecom customer attributes such as tenure, contract, service usage, billing, and charges.

Machine Learning Models

The system evaluates multiple models including Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, SVM, KNN, and XGBoost when available.

Workflow

Data ingestion, preprocessing, feature engineering, model training, evaluation, prediction, and business recommendations.
